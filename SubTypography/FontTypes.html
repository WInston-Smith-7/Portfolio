Types of fonts

Display fonts, fonts meant to be used for titles, logos, headers and more to call attention and be distinct. Some of my favorite display fonts include punk fonts that typically fall under the sub category of “Grunge”. An example of this is the font “Very Very Punk” found at: daFont

Very Very Punk Font

Another core type of typeface are body fonts. One of my favorites being alegreya. Its a serif typface that I particularly adore using for TTRPG dungeons. Its many font weights provide for ample options to give context to different parts of text.
