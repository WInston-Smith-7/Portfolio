Photo Restoration

Original Photo

Restored Photo

Writeup

Project goal

The goal of this project was to restore this photo by healing away the damage to the photograph. Removing dust, scratches, flakes creases, and punctures.

Assets

A single base photo named “JE_DT.PNG"

The process

I created a healing layer above the base image and went to clear away the white flecks using the spot healing brush tool. I then took care of what seemed to be a puncture in the photo on Epstein’s vest that was too large for the spot healing. I used the healing brush tool and selected a nearby spot to place over the damaged part. I then worked on the creases on the photo with the spot healing in some parts and clone brush in other parts. I then darkened the photo to tone down the brightness of the flash.

Difficulties

I largely feel the photo restoration is complete meets the goals I set out for it and I had little to no difficulty accomplishing it.
